About these reports

Both reports share a common structure focused on a single question: why is a regional HPI not sufficient for individual loan collateral assessment, how does the local factor model correct for that, and by how much?

Each report covers: (1) the limitation of regional HPI alone, (2) how the local factor model adds postcode and property-type precision, (3) the quantified improvement in numbers, (4) the 85% confidence haircut calibration, and (5) out-of-sample validation on 2022–2024 transactions.

Models are calibrated on 15M+ Land Registry repeat-sale pairs and tested on unseen transactions from 2022 onwards — a period including the rate-driven correction — to satisfy the Basel 3.1 SA-CR requirement for conservatively assessed collateral values.
